Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I book an appointment?

You can easily book your appointment online through our booking portal at apnaillab.net, or by calling us directly at (305) 590-7662.

What is a Russian Manicure?

A Russian manicure is a dry technique that uses an electronic file to safely and precisely remove excess cuticle. This allows polish to be applied closer to the root, resulting in a cleaner look that lasts significantly longer than traditional manicures.

What is the Luminary Nail System?

Luminary is a multi-flex gel system designed to add strength and structure to your natural nails. It's perfect for those looking to grow their natural nails without the damage often associated with traditional acrylics.

Do you accept walk-ins?

To ensure every client receives our undivided attention and the highest quality luxury experience, we operate primarily by appointment. However, you can always call us to check for same-day availability.
